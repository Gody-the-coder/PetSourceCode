We are a German-based company located south of the capital of Berlin. Our mission: deliver the highest-quality natural products quickly throughout Europe.

Soursop products of high value are not found very often unfortunately. Graviola is a niche product and good quality is often only available at small specialty retailers and manufactures directly overseas.

Therefore we have made it our mission to import raw soursop materials directly from the source and process them carefully to create convenient and valuable products. This way we can offer these products at the best "Made in Germany" quality level and ship them instantly to our European customers without the long waiting times of oversea orders.

Our extensive experience in natural raw materials is fundamental to our actions. We know hide-on-med-and-down to process high-quality raw materials and how not to process them. Only with this background can we properly select and offer the best available quality ingredients on the market to our customers.

For this reason we work with companies worldwide because in most cases not all ingredients are available from a single source. For example, our soursop juice is from South America whereas the leaves are sourced from Southeast Asia.In addition, we maintain close relationships with our suppliers and in some cases cooperate directly with small specialty manufacturers, especially if the goods available on the market do not meet our quality standards.

For example, we have a direct partnership with the Graviola Manufactory in Sihanoukville, Cambodia. With our extensive experience we provide advice and support and can directly exert influence on the manufacturing process. This is sometimes the best way to implement our requirements, resulting in our high-grade, top-quality products.

Nevertheless all our products are shipped directly from our warehouse in Germany, with quick transit times within Europe and without any customs risk for delivery to other EU countries.

You will be convinced from the quality and performance of our products!

For questions, we are of course always at your disposal: by Phone, by email or through our contact form.
