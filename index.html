Soursop dried fruits are a sweet power snack for in between meals. A delicious treat for the whole family.

Triple-concentrated fruit power: For 1 kilo of our dried fruits, more then 3 kilos of fresh fruits needs to be processed. In this way, 750g equals 2 full liters of Soursop juice.

Thanks to a unique drying process, the soursop fruit with its exotic taste und its valuable ingredients is finally available to us.

The ripened fruits are peeled and the seeds removed. Afterwards, the fruit pulp is processed through a unique drying process which gently extracts the high water content, while preserving the valuable ingredients. At the end of the drying process, similar to common dried fruits, the dried pulp is sugared and sulfurized. In this way, the dried soursop fruit is durable for some months.
